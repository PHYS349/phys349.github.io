# Purdue Resources
## Academic Integrity & AI Policy
Purdue prohibits academic dishonesty. Report concerns to [integrity@purdue.edu](mailto:integrity@purdue.edu).

* **Authorized AI Use:** You are encouraged to use AI (Gemini, ChatGPT) as a **tutor** or **debugger**.
* **Unauthorized AI Use:** You may **not** copy-paste AI output as your submission. You are responsible for the accuracy of your work.
* **Consequences:** Academic misconduct will result in a **zero on the assignment** and referral to the Office of Student Rights and Responsibilities (OSRR).

## Nondiscrimination

## Accessibility (ADA & DRC)
We are committed to making all course materials accessible under **ADA Title II**. If you require accommodations:
1.  **Contact:** [Disability Resource Center (DRC)](mailto:drc@purdue.edu) at 765-494-1247.
2.  **Accommodations:** Release your Course Accommodation Letter (CAL) to me as soon as possible.
3.  **Exams:** You must schedule exams with **Purdue Testing Services** at least **4 days (96 hours)** in advance.
